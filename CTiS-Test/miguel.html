Miguel's Ethical Reflection:


In my reflection that overall during lockdown the practical implication of autonomous robotics has provided greatly positive effects on society around the world, closely regarding the way contactless interaction have improved ways to benefits society. Seen in how autonomous robotics are used transport/delivery via autonomous vehicles that of goods can be done with no one to one contact with another human. Thus, improving safety from covid-19 transmissions. These are explored in these resources. Where according to Liu 2021 journal, 'Hercules Autonomous Vehicle' a form of transport contactless goods in china with multiple autonomous Vehicle’s had been deployed showing which had shown result of these benefits. So, based this and many other resources that have recommend that same which have various benefits I believe that autonomous robotics have shown amount more benefits than negative effects like malfunctions or cost to society. Especially, as many different industries have used autonomous robotics in one way shows just how its production/quality and variability in different circumstance can be widely beneficial has it has shown.
