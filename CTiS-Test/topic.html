Why are autonomous robots important during Covid-19? A robot can be described as a machine that is able to complete the same tasks normally done by a human. Generally five types of robots: pre-programmed, humanoid, autonomous, teleoperated, and augmenting. Our focus topic will be about autonomous robots especially during the Covid-19 pandemic.
